Description

House MD is a popular American medical drama television series that aired on Fox from 2004 to 2012. It follows the story of the brilliant but unconventional diagnostician, Dr. Gregory House, played by Hugh Laurie, who leads a team of physicians at the fictional Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital. The show's central character, Dr. House, is known for his sarcastic wit, acerbic personality, and unorthodox methods of treating patients. He often clashes with his colleagues and superiors, including his best friend Dr. James Wilson, played by Robert Sean Leonard, and hospital administrator Dr. Lisa Cuddy, played by Lisa Edelstein. Each episode of House MD typically centers around a difficult medical case that the team must solve. Dr. House and his team use their expertise, intuition, and unconventional methods to diagnose and treat the patient's ailments, often leading to unexpected twists and turns in the plot. Throughout the series, Dr. House also struggles with chronic pain and addiction to pain medication, which adds a layer of complexity to his character and relationships with others. House MD was highly praised for its writing, acting, and portrayal of medical cases, and it remains a popular and influential show in the medical drama genre.

Trivia

  1. "House M.D." premiered on November 16, 2004 and ran for eight seasons until May 21, 2012.

  2. The show was created by David Shore and produced by Paul Attanasio.

  3. The character of Dr. Gregory House was inspired by Sherlock Holmes.

  4. The show was filmed on location in a real hospital, the Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital, in Princeton, New Jersey.

  5. Hugh Laurie, who played Dr. Gregory House, is actually British and had to learn an American accent for the role.

  6. The show was a critical and commercial success, receiving multiple Emmy nominations and winning numerous awards, including two Golden Globes for Hugh Laurie.

  7. Each episode of the show is named after a medical diagnosis or condition.

  8. Dr. House's team of doctors changed over the course of the show, but some of the most notable characters included Dr. Eric Foreman, Dr. Allison Cameron, Dr. Robert Chase, and Dr. Remy "Thirteen" Hadley.

  9. The character of Dr. James Wilson, House's best friend, was played by Robert Sean Leonard.

  10. The show's theme song is "Teardrop" by Massive Attack.

  11. The show's medical consultant, Dr. Lisa Sanders, also writes a column for The New York Times Magazine called "Diagnosis."

  12. The character of Dr. House was known for his sarcastic and cynical personality, often making humorous and biting comments to his colleagues and patients.

  13. Many of the medical cases featured on the show were based on real-life cases that the writers researched.

  14. The show's finale, which aired on May 21, 2012, was watched by over 8 million viewers.

  15. "House M.D." is still popular among fans and is often cited as one of the best medical dramas of all time.
